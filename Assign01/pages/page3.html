Favorite Linux Distros

  1. Arch - Fun to to mess with if you want to get your hands dirty

  2. Kali - For the inner script kiddie and wanna be hackers

  3. Mint - Get up and going fast

  4. Manjaro - Arch without the pain

  5. NixOS - You have a lot of computers or switch computers a lot

  6. Ubuntu - Blissfully easy to use

  7. Puppy - Very small, good for recovery

  8. Rescatux - Stuff hit the fan

  9. PopOS - Start here if you havent used linux

  10. EndevourOS - Somewhere between Arch and Manjaro, Pretty Nice
